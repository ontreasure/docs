Follow these steps to build your first event page on Treasure!

<Steps>
  <Step title="Basic Info">
    To start, you will be prompted to add information critical to your event, such as your:

    * Event Name

    * Venue Details

    * Event Poster

    * Description and Dates

    ![](/images/image70.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Ticket and Table Tiers">
    Next, you add information around the pricing of your tickets and tables, as well as additional questions for vendors. For the vendor process, you also add information about where/when to check-in along with your terms & conditions.

    ![](/images/vendor-app.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Additional Information">
    Finally, you can add:

    * Tags that provide your audience context around what vendors at your event will be selling

    * Special guest information to highlight guests from your&#x20;
  </Step>
</Steps>
