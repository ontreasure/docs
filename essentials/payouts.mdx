---
title: "Payouts"
description: "Set your preferred payout method to initiate payouts from Treasure"
---

This guide will walk you through the steps for setting your payout method and monitoring as payouts arrive.

<Note>
  Payout cadence changes based on your account's plan. See [pricing](https://www.ontreasure.com/pricing) for a breakdown of the different plans and their benefits
</Note>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to Payouts">
    From one of your host dashboard, click the Payouts (piggy bank icon) button on the lefthand menu. You can also access this page directly [here](https://www.ontreasure.com/host/payouts/methods).

    ![Screenshot 2025-11-15 at 1.38.23 AM.png](/images/Screenshot2025-11-15at1.38.23AM.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set Your Method">
    From the payouts page, switch to the "Payout Methods" and then click "Connect with Stripe" to connect your bank account and start receiving payouts. 

    <Info>
      _Note that your bank is not connected until you complete all the entire Stripe Connect process_
    </Info>
    ![Screenshot 2025-11-15 at 1.38.50 AM.png](/images/Screenshot2025-11-15at1.38.50AM.png)

    After going through the steps on Stripe's Connect platform, your account is now ready to receive payouts. You can check your [payouts page](https://www.ontreasure.com/host/payouts) at any time as your payouts come in to keep a record of the funds you received from Treasure across events.
  </Step>
</Steps>
