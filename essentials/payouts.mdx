---
title: "Payouts"
description: "Set your preferred payout method to initiate payouts from Treasure"
---

This guide will walk you through the steps for setting your payout method and monitoring as payouts arrive.&#x20;

<Note>
  Payout cadence changes based on your account's plan. See [pricing](https://www.ontreasure.com/pricing) for a breakdown of the different plans and their benefits
</Note>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to Payouts">
    From one of your event's dashboard pages, click the payouts (piggy bank icon) button on the lefthand menu

    ![](/images/event-dash.png)

    ![](/images/payouts-nav.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Set Your Method">
    From the payouts page, select "click to update" on the card in the center of the page

    ![](/images/payouts-method.png)

    Select your preferred payout method, enter the username associated with it (*i.e. your username on Venmo or phone number on Zelle*), and remember to save the information you add!

    ![](/images/pref-method-payout.png)
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Check>
  Your payment method is now set. Check back to the page as your payouts come in to keep a record of the funds you received from Treasure for a specific event.&#x20;

  ![](/images/payouts-pop.png)
</Check>
